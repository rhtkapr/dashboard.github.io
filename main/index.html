Our Story Behind Our Story!

In July 2017, three friends founded a company called V-Geekers with a mission to help students discover and refine their personal style. Starting with just a small investment and based out of a few square foot apartment, we tried developing stories every week.
From the past few years, they have witnessed students around the world spent 50 million hours on the site, which is more than 2 million days or 5.7 thousand years. And, what were students consuming in all that time? Nothing fruitful. Our content ranges from developing Websites and Android Applications to Graphics Designing from the Out-of-the-box Thinking, creating simple yet Fruitful Software Tools, advice from industry leaders on how to be a badass in the workplace, and more— all through V-Geekers's signature lens of optimism and creativity. Our content is global and can be found anywhere, from your desktop to your Smartphones, videos and IRL through events. And with that, V-Geekers has become the number-one destination for students who crave style and substance, thought-provoking conversations, and the inspiration to live a more creative life.
